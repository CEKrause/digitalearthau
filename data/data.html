NBAR and NBAR-T¶

NBAR stands for Nadir-corrected BRDF Adjusted Reflectance. It is the surface reflectance product in

It has the bands:

Name Description
blue Blue
green Green
red Red
nir Near Infra-red
swir1 Short-wave Infra-red 1
swir2 Short-wave Infra-red 2

Name Description
